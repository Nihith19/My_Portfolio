Project Overview

Alanati Pindivantalu is an online platform selling authentic homemade Telugu sweets, snacks and bulk orders for weddings and festivals.

Problem Statement

Users struggled with product discoverability, bulk order pricing visibility, and a confusing checkout process.

Goal

Create a culturally resonant, user-friendly experience that improves navigation, product visibility and checkout flow.
